Top 5 Must-Visit Places in Kazakhstan

1

Bayterek Tower (Astana)

A symbol of Kazakhstan’s independence, the Bayterek Tower represents a mythical golden egg held by a sacred bird from Kazakh folklore. The observation deck offers breathtaking panoramic views of Astana’s skyline.

2

Charyn Canyon

Often called "Kazakhstan’s Grand Canyon," Charyn Canyon is a natural wonder with stunning red rock formations shaped over millions of years. A paradise for hikers and photographers.

3

Big Almaty Lake

Nestled in the mountains, this glacial lake changes colors depending on the season, from turquoise to deep blue. A perfect spot for hiking and nature lovers.

4

Medeu Ice Rink

The highest-altitude ice rink in the world, Medeu is a legendary skating destination with crystal-clear mountain air and stunning views.

5

Almaty Green Bazaar

A vibrant market where visitors can taste traditional Kazakh food, buy local spices, and experience authentic culture.
